    TARKIBGASAZAR

    Fanavaran Azar Gas Combination Company is a manufacturing and trading company in the field of gas and related equipment.

    Fanavaran Gas Combination Company is one of the leading suppliers in the field of industrial, laboratory and medical gases and has been supplying argon, helium, nitrogen, hydrogen and other pure gases at high pressures since 2014.

    The experienced production team is proud of its ability to manufacture a variety of gas mixtures in ppm and percentage concentrations such as calibration gas mixtures, welding, cutting, fire extinguishing, standard gas and is the exclusive manufacturer of sub-specialized combined gas refining lasers in Iran. And is a member of the National Union of Medical and Industrial Gases.
